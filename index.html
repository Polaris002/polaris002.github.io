A song filled with new words,a cup filled with wine The bower is last year's,the weather is as fine. Will last year reappera as the sun on decline? Deeply I sigh for the fallen flowers in vain; Vaguely I seem to know the swallows come again. In fragrant garden path alone I still remain. 一曲新词酒一杯，去年天气旧亭台。夕阳西下几时回？ 无可奈何花落去，似曾相识燕归来，小院香径独徘徊。
